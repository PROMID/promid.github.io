Registration

Participation in the PROMID 2025 shared task requires registration. Please click on the link below to register

Register for PROMID 2025

There is no registration fee for participating in the shared task itself. However, participants who wish to submit their working notes and present their work at FIRE 2025 may need to register for the main conference. A limited number of student travel grants might be available, details of which will be announced closer to the conference dates.

Important Notes:

  • Please register with an email address that you check regularly. All communications will be sent to this address.
  • Each participating team should register only once. Please nominate a team leader for communication purposes.
  • Each participating team can have a maximum of four members.
  • The registration deadline is 31st July 2025.
  • By registering, you agree to adhere to the task guidelines and data usage agreement (to be provided with dataset).

After registration, you will be added to our mailing list for updates, dataset release announcements, and submission platform details.

If you have any questions, please contact us at promid@googlegroups.com.
